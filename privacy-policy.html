Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Ayat Finder ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details.

1.2 Location Data

We may request access to your location data to provide accurate prayer timings and Qiblah compass functionality. This location data is used solely within the app and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide and Improve Our Services

We use your location data to provide accurate prayer timings and Qiblah directions. The location data is processed locally on your device and is not shared with anyone.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Your location data remains on your device and is not sent to any external servers or parties.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

5.1 Location Data

You can enable or disable location services through your mobile device settings. Please note that disabling location services may affect the functionality of certain features of the app, such as prayer timings and Qiblah compass.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all age groups, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users of any age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
